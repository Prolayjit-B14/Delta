IoT Components

Essential hardware components used in IoT projects.

🔹 Sensors

Sensors play a crucial role in IoT projects, collecting real-time environmental data. Common IoT sensors include:

  • 📍 DHT11/DHT22 – Temperature & Humidity
  • 📍 BMP180 – Pressure Sensor
  • 📍 MQ-135 – Air Quality Sensor
  • 📍 HC-SR04 – Ultrasonic Distance Sensor

🔹 Microcontrollers

Microcontrollers act as the brain of IoT devices. Popular choices:

  • 🖥️ Arduino Uno – Beginner-friendly
  • 🖥️ ESP32 – Dual-core & Bluetooth
  • 🖥️ Raspberry Pi – Advanced IoT projects

🔹 Communication Modules

IoT devices rely on communication modules to send and receive data:

  • 📶 ESP8266 – Wi-Fi
  • 📶 NRF24L01 – Wireless
  • 📶 LoRa – Long-range communication
  • 📶 GSM/GPRS – Cellular connectivity

🔹 Power Supply

Reliable power sources for IoT devices:

  • 🔋 Li-Ion Batteries
  • 🔋 Solar Panels
  • 🔋 DC Power Adapters
  • 🔋 Power Banks

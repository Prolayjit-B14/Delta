What is Delta?

Delta is a vibrant community of budding electronics enthusiasts, formed by students, dedicated to showcasing innovative projects. Our mission is to inspire others by sharing our work, which ranges from simple circuits to complex systems. Explore our projects, learn from our experiences, and join us in celebrating creativity and innovation in electronics.
